Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply register as a user, search for the service you need, choose a professional based on their profile and ratings, and click "Book Now" with your preferred time.

Is my payment secure?

Yes! We use an integrated secure payment gateway simulation to ensure your details are always protected during transactions.

How can I join as a service provider?

Click "Join Now" and select "Service Provider" in the registration form. Complete your profile and start receiving booking requests instantly.
